A Birthday Card for Contact Improvisation

Dear C.I.,

Here's a list of 25 gifts you've given me:

Thank you for:

  • a network of companions all over the world
  • for resisting definition, codification and doctrine
  • for not making us dress up
  • for the desire and discipline of the practitioners to bring language to this dance form
  • for not being trendy
  • for a strong and supple body
  • for many good C.I. performances, and some bad ones
  • for the adrenelinnnnnnnnne

Thank you for:

  • the generosity of the people who teach this form
  • for not having to learn any steps
  • for the challenge of dancing with people of all different abilities, including those in wheelchairs and the blind
  • for being fertile ground for metaphor
  • for being a dance inclusive of regional, temporal and personal variations
  • for the friends, colleagues and lovers
  • for deepening our capacity for pleasure
  • for always being an incomplete form- so people can complete it with themselves

Thank you for:

  • the range of touching and being touched
  • for the reverence dancing with men and the rapture dancing with women
  • for not having certifications, belts, diplomas, ranks, and hazing initiations
  • for the laughter
  • for getting to be around (and around) such wonderful bodies
  • for being my living, my support, my lively-hood
  • for often inspiring humility by how inclusive and magnanimous you are
  • for being such a spontaneous yet consistent partner
  • for keeping me so close to the worlds of paradox and mystery

Happy 25th Birthday C.I. I love you,

Martin Keogh
