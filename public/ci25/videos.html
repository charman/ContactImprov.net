Videos collected at CI25

This table lists the videos that were brought to CI25. They were available there in the reference room, but afterwards, most or all were taken back by their owners. I have placed this list on line as documentation of what was there, as it gives some idea of what pieces of contact history have been documented. But there is no "library" for contact video, and I regret I am not able to assist you in locating these videos or their owners.

The "tape number" (column 1) the number assigned by the video curator (Sheri Cohen) at CI25. When more than one segment was on the same physical tape, the second number (#) gives the sequence on the tape.
